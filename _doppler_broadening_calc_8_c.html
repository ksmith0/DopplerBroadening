Plots the broadening due to Doppler effects including energy shift, opening angle, and spread in beta values. Also, plots the individual components.

Parameters
[in]energyMeVThe energy of the emitted gamma-ray in MeV.
[in]betaThe fraction of the speed of light of incoming beam.
[in]dThetaDegThe angular coverage of the detector in degrees.
[in]resolutionConstThe constant term in the 1/sqrt(e) energy resolution function. This should be in units of sqrt(MeV).
[in]dBetaThe width of the distribution of the beta values.
Returns
Nothing.
